I'm a former Manufacturing Engineer turned Software Engineer residing in Toronto, Canada.

I joined Tesla as the company was leaping into automation for the assembly line. My work there exposed me to high-level robotic programming and deepened my interest in Software Engineering.

At Pen-Link Inc, I contributed to developing Frontend and Backend components for Geotime Enterprise and Geotime Live applications, leveraging diverse technologies.

I built Trellis, a deployment pipeline for serverless applications.

Trellis

Trellis is a low-config, CICD pipeline for teams who develop serverless applications on AWS. It handles the complexity of setting up automated deployment pipelines for its users, making it suitable for teams who want to spend less time on operations and more time on feature development.

Trellis gives users the ability to deploy, promote, teardown, rollback and test their applications on the cloud with a simple click.
